Registration Information

SBP-BRiMS Program and Special Issue Committee members should contact Dr. Kathleen M. Carley at sbpbrims@andrew.cmu.edu for registration information. All people receiving either travel grants, graduate student consortium grants or diversity scholarships must still register and pay for the conference and any tutorials they are interested in attending. See the Scholarship Page for more information. If your paper was accepted, at least one of the author's must register at the same time that the camera ready version or the paper is submitted, or the paper will be pulled from the proceedings.There are NO refunds on registration.

NOTE: When you register there is only one registration, general. The registration site states that discounts (student discount and early bird discounts will be assessed at checkout and also gives the amount. You simply need to choose general registration and then go to "check out" to see the final amount or actually FINISH the registration. You can always log out and pay later but you have to complete the registration process to see the discounts.

NOTE: Papers will be pulled from the proceedings if at least one author does not register and pay.
NOTE: If your name is on two papers - you can present only one and your co-author will need to present the other.
NOTE: All program committee members who attend need to register and pay. There is no fee waiver.

  • Early General Registration (price increases June 20 at 5pm): $355.00
  • Early Student Registration (price increases June 20 at 5pm): $280.00

Regular registration starts June 20, 2020 at 5PM EDT.

  • General Registration: $505.00
  • Student Registration: $355.00
